QUALITY ASSURANCE

Our Company is SFC accredited for the fabrication and erection of structures up to and including Construction Category C3 (CC3) under AS/NZS 5131. We are certified in accordance with AS/NZS ISO 3834 Part 2, with our welding coordination team qualified to ISO 3834.5 for the management of welding structural and reinforcing steel in compliance with AS/NZS 1554 Part 1 & Part 3. Our welders are qualified to ISO 9606.1 and AS/NZS 2980, with our site erection team trained and qualified by SCNZ to supervise the installation of High Strength Bolts, with specific training in TB and TF tensioning. We also have an in-house welding supervisor and inspector qualified to supervise and carry out formal welding inspections under AS2214 and IIW IWI WT1 joint qualifications.
